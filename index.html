Letters I Cannot Send Yet

held together with pink(ish) roses.

For the love that i cannot show yet.

A notebook for letters I cannot give yet, and a memory lane for all the time between meeting and finally being allowed to love out loud.

I made this because some feelings are too real to ignore but too early to speak out loud, also I needed somewhere gentle to keep every letter, every memory, every version of missing you without losing any of it. This is my way of holding on carefully, day by day, until the three years are over and these pages no longer have to stay private.

Notebook

Gallery / Memory Lane

A timeline from the day we met to the present. New memories are only added with Create Memory, and existing ones are edited with the pencil button.
